General Secretary Hmuahnak ca

Duhdawt Mi Laicin Seempi Unau zaten Khrih hmin in nan kut kan lo kai. Pathian lam hruainak in CBANA Tin-Jubilee kan thleng. CABNA hram tohnak hmun “Atlanta Chin Baptist Church, GA” ah Pathian hnen lungawi thusimnak Jubilee dar tum ding kan si. Zumtu unau nan zaten Pathian hnen lungawi thu sim tlang dingah kan lo sawm. A tikcu hi May 25-27, 2018 a si ding. Tin-Jubilee darkhing awn mawi te thawn “International Chin Theological Seminar(ICTS)” bible tlawng siseh,Falam Bible aw ih ngai theih dingih ret mi Pathian hnen hlan kainak siseh neih a si ding. Thuthangtha thawn siseh, hla thawn siseh, Pathian hmin sunlawinak hrangah bible zohlo zuam awknak, pakzai, hlalaam, hlarem zuamawknak, mipi zaten Haleluijah Chorus saknak pawl thawn can hman a si ding. Leitlun huap laicin seempi zaten sawm nan si. Atlanta phairawn, USA ah zaten tongaw ding in timtuah cio hram uhsi. Khrih ah Nan unau Sa Tin Lal

ACBC Pastor Hmuahnak ca

ACBC Pastor welcome speech Thihnak in a thosal tu kan Bawipa Jesuh le Atlanta Chin Baptist hmin in nan kut neem kan lo kai. Pathian in rem a ti le tukum May 25 in 27 tiang a vei ( 10 ) nak Chin Baptist Association khawmpi cu Atlanta, Georgia, USA ah nei ding kan si. Hmunkip ih ummi zumtu nan zaten Pathian thangthat biak-khawmnak ah in ra telpi ve ding in Bawi Khrih hmin in kan lo sawm!. Thlarau lam ih kan pa le pawl in thuthangtha simnak le zirhnak an nei ding ih tulai Pathian ih a hman rero mi kan hlasak thiam pawl khal an tel ding a si. Kan miphun thlarau, thinlung le taksa damnak dingah le Bawipai ram a kauh sinsin nakdingah Pathian thangthat tlangnak le thu le hla ruahrel tlangnak kan nei ding. Isaiah 54: 2 “ Na puanthlam ( puan inn ) kau ter aw” timi thulu hmang in khawmpi kan hmang ding. Kan zumnak puanthlam, duhhdawtnak puanthlam le kan peknak puanthlam pawl kau ter in CBANA khawmpi ih ratel ding ah Bawi Khrih hmin in kan lo sawm !
