General Secretary Hmuahnak ca

Duhdawt Mi Laicin Seempi Unau zaten

Khrih hmin in nan kut kan lo kai. Pathian lam hruainak in CBANA Tin-Jubilee kan thleng. CABNA hram tohnak hmun  “Atlanta Chin Baptist Church, GA” ah  Pathian hnen lungawi thusimnak Jubilee dar tum ding kan si. Zumtu unau nan zaten Pathian hnen lungawi thu sim tlang dingah kan lo sawm. A tikcu  hi May 25-27, 2018 a si ding.  
Tin-Jubilee darkhing awn mawi te thawn “International Chin Theological Seminar(ICTS)” bible tlawng siseh,Falam Bible  aw ih ngai theih dingih ret mi Pathian hnen hlan kainak siseh neih a si ding.  
Thuthangtha  thawn siseh, hla thawn siseh, Pathian hmin sunlawinak hrangah  bible zohlo  zuam awknak, pakzai, hlalaam, hlarem zuamawknak, ,mipi zaten Haleluijah Chorus saknak pawl thawn can hman a si ding.  
Leitlun huap laicin seempi zaten  sawm nan si. Atlanta phairawn, USA ah zaten tongaw ding in timtuah cio  hram uhsi.  
 Khrih ah Nan unau
Sa Tin Lal
